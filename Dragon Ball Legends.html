Lesson 2:technicalities

There are 4 type of cards that will rotate around your deck and it is arbitrary based. Here are the 4 cards:

    Strike
    Blast
    Special skill
    Special move

Card usages(hover me)

Usages of strike

  • only for combos, not for getting priority, but it is possible to full screen strike if aware of patterns of opponent.
  • for farming dragon balls.
  • mid range strike if aware of oppononent's pattern

Usages of blast

  • Main source of engaging the opponent
  • Mid range blast if aware of pattern but high risk
  • Long range blast is less risky and unable to get pv

Usages of special skill

  • Frame trap
  • Defend from long ranged blast
  • To add more reassurance to identifying patterns by frame trapping

Usages of Special move

  • Defend oneself from long ranged blast
  • Punish mid range strike

The rate of the cards drawn is relative to what cards the character holds.
